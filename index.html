Privacy Policy

The Last Witness · Last updated: 31 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you play The Last Witness (the “Game”).

1. Information Collection

The Last Witness does not request or collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.

The Game may automatically collect limited, anonymous technical data, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Game version
  • Crash and error logs
  • Anonymous gameplay statistics

2. Use of Information

Collected data is used solely to ensure stability, fix errors, and improve the overall game experience. No data is used for advertising, profiling, or marketing.

3. Third‑Party Services

The Game may rely on third‑party services such as distribution platforms (e.g. Google Play or Steam) and crash reporting tools. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Cookies

The Last Witness does not directly use cookies. Platform providers may use cookies according to their own policies.

5. Data Retention

Anonymous technical data is retained only for as long as necessary and may be deleted or anonymized after analysis.

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are taken to protect collected data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under the age of 13, and no personal data is knowingly collected from children.

8. Your Rights

If you are located in the European Union, you may have rights under GDPR, including the right to request access to or deletion of data, where applicable.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
