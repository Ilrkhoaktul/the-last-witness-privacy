Privacy Policy

The Last Witness – Last updated: 31 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use The Last Witness (the “Game”).

1. Information Collection

The Last Witness does not request or collect personal information such as name, email address, or contact details.

The Game may automatically collect limited technical and anonymous data, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Game version
  • Crash and error logs
  • Anonymous gameplay statistics

2. Use of Information

Collected data is used solely to:

  • Ensure the Game functions properly
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Improve game performance and stability

No data is used for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

3. Third‑Party Services

The Game may rely on third‑party services such as distribution platforms (e.g. Google Play or Steam) and crash reporting tools. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Cookies

The Last Witness does not directly use cookies. Third‑party services or platforms may use cookies according to their own policies.

5. Data Retention

Anonymous technical data is retained only as long as necessary for analysis and troubleshooting, after which it may be deleted or anonymized.

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are taken to protect collected data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under the age of 13, and no personal data is knowingly collected from children.

8. Your Rights

If you are located in the European Union, you may have rights under GDPR, including the right to request access to or deletion of data, where applicable.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
